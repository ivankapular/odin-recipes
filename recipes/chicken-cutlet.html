Chicken Cutlet Recipe

Description

Juicy chicken cutlets seasoned to perfection, a bread crumb coating, fried to crispy golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • Italian bread crumbs - homemade or store bought
  • grated Parmesan cheese
  • dry white wine
  • garlic
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Slice each chicken breast in two or three horizontally.
  2. Using a meat mallet, pound the chicken breast between two sheets of plastic until thin.
  3. Whisk together egg, white wine, crushed garlic, salt and pepper.
  4. Add chicken cutlets turning each over to coat in egg mixture.
  5. Combine Italian breadcrumbs with parmesan cheese.
  6. Lift chicken breast pieces out one by one to coat in bread crumbs.
  7. Turn chicken over in the bread crumbs and press to stick the crumbs.
  8. Shallow fry in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through.
